Thank you for visiting “Gamerz of Taft”, a news and entertainment site about games and things serious gamers care about. Our main goal is to inform you, our readers, and most importantly, to please you. We aim to be an inclusive site for gamers of any ethnicity, gender or sexual orientation. For any suggestions, feel free to email us anytime!





























Founded in 2017, Gamerz of Taft is a new video gaming destination offering video game enthusiasts high-quality, innovative and exclusive content with a considered approach to its audience. It was created by a group of students from De La Salle University, known as the Potato Banana Keyboard Warriors on August 20, 2017.

The name was based from the popular  American fantasy drama television series, created by David Benioff and D. B. Weiss, the “Games of Thrones” mostly known as “G.O.T.” The makers changed the word “Thrones” into “Taft” (a place in Manila City where the group’s school was located) and used the word “Gamerz” to further emphasize the millennial gamers. On August 17, 2017, the website was officially known as the Gamerz of Taft.

The Gamerz of Taft aims to catch and praise the contemporary video gaming lifestyle and the various worldwide computer game culture by the most interesting articles and important segments created by computer game experts.
